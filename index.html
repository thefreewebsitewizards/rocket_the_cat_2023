About Rocket

I am an adorable, food motivated, 4 year old chonky boy who loves meeting new friends and fostering long lasting, engaging friendships with my followers! I was rescued by my Meowm as a very young kitten after being abandoned in the middle of a busy road ...... needless to say our bond was instantaneous and we've been inseparable ever since. She manages my account and loves posting daily content of me and my adventures on Instagram. I love trying out new cat friendly products and foods and sharing my honest reviews with my growing community of cat lovers. I would love to make you and your brand a part of my family! *
